Here's a little bit about us
Methods is a band which pioneers in the art of entertainment through engaging the audience by a variety of musical performances.
In every event, we strive to provide the best live musical performances through our creativity, dynamic energy, inclusivity, and aim for perfection.

Methods is a result of the dedication, hard work and passion each one of us has towards music and for entertainment along with the combination of
following our dream of doing what we love to give the best to our audience and redefining the live entertainment industry.

Each one of us brings a unique aspect to the band which blends to create quality entertainment and appeals to the younger and older generations alike,
be it dancing the night away or soothing their hearts with our captivating performances.

We are performers with over 5 years of experience and having done more than 500 performances in Sri Lanka.
Our performances range a span of events which include corporate events,weddings, festivals, band competitions, tv and online shows and other special events.
